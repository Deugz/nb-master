My name is Vincent Deguin and I am an Astrophysics PhD student at the Open University (as of 1st of August 2022). My PhD focus on Experimenting with the earliest stages of planet formation and I am particulary interested in understanding the key role that ice (coating interstellar dust grains) plays in that respect (both from a physical and chemical point of view). I have an insatiable appetite for Open Science, that is unfortunately overlooked by the scientific community. This web-site is an attempt to make the outputs of my scientific journey as open, accesible and reproducible as possible, and is dived in 3 main sections:

Research ✨

A place where I intend to share my research outputs

Teaching 🚀

Teaching material based on what I learned during my Scientific journey.

Blog 💥

I have never been so keen in keeping my mouth shut, mostly when what I want to say is related to injustice. The Scientific world is full of it so here is a place where I will share my say.
